What is Vegetable Prices Prediction?

Vegetable price prediction is the process of using historical datasets and statistical models to forecast the future prices of various vegetables. This predictive analysis involves analyzing factors such as seasonal trends, weather conditions, demand-supply dynamics, economic indicators, and other relevant variables that influence vegetable prices.
